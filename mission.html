Our Mission

To promote diversity, provide opportunities and benefit UR students by facilitating the exchange of information between China and the UR community.

Our Vision

We regularly organize social activities, conduct seminars and symposiums, and promote Chinese culture through media such as arts, music, and sports. We also seek opportunities to coorperate with other student organizations on campus and organize formal, large‐scale events.
